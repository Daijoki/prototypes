This lack of information makes it difficult to assess the dataset's representativeness and potential biases. Will you proceed with this dataset or switch to the ASL Citizen Dataset?

Outcome - Proceeding with Unlabeled Dataset

You've decided to proceed with the Unlabeled Dataset. As you continue your exploration, you encounter various challenges and make important decisions. Good luck!

Uncovering the ASL Citizen Dataset

Outcome - Reevaluating Dataset Choice

You've decided to reevaluate your dataset choice due to the identified risks in the ASL Citizen Dataset. It's essential to ensure the dataset's integrity and representativeness for your sign language recognition model.

AI Model Development

You've decided to move forward with the ASL Citizen Dataset, embracing the challenges it presents. During testing, your model struggles to accurately recognize signs from older individuals and those with non-standard sign language dialects. Reflect on whether the DNP Label predicted these issues and think about strategies to address them.

Reflect and Address Bias

Consider different strategies to address the bias issues:

Update Training Data

Great choice! By updating the training data to include more diverse samples, you can improve the model's performance and ensure better accuracy. However, consider the potential impact. Meet Jane, a 70-year-old woman who relies on sign language for communication. If your model was trained primarily on data from young, adult signers, it might struggle to recognize Jane's signs. Jane might feel frustrated and excluded, underscoring the importance of diverse training data.

Apply Data Augmentation Techniques

Excellent! Data augmentation techniques can help to increase the diversity and size of your dataset, potentially improving the model's performance. However, remember that these techniques should be used carefully to avoid introducing new biases. Consider Tom, a 10-year-old boy learning sign language. If your model was trained primarily on adult signers and didn't include enough data from children, it might fail to recognize Tom's signs accurately. This could lead to frustration and hinder his learning process, highlighting the need for careful data augmentation.

Adjust Model Parameters

Good thinking! Adjusting model parameters can help to optimize the model's performance. However, this alone may not fully address the bias issue. It's often beneficial to combine this strategy with others, such as updating the training data or applying data augmentation techniques.
